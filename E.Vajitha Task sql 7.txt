OrderID	  CustomerName	  Products
101	  Ajith kumar	  Laptop, Mouse
102	  Vikram  	  Mobile, Charger

Problem: Multiple products are stored in a single column (repeating group).

First Normal Form (1NF)
OrderID	  CustomerName	        Product
101	  Ajith Kumar	        Laptop
101	  Ajith Kumar	        Mouse
102	  Vikram	        Mobile
102	  Vikram	        Charger
Second Normal Form (2NF)

Split into two tables:

Orders

OrderID  	CustomerName
101	          Ajith Kumar
102	          Vikram

OrderDetails

OrderID  	Product
101	        Laptop
101	        Mouse
102	        Mobile
102	        Charger

Now all non-key attributes depend on the whole primary key.
